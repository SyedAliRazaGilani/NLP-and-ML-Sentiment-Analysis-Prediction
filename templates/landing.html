What this analysis shows (under the hood)

  • NLP preprocessing: regex clean → lowercase → tokenize → stopword removal → Porter stemming.
  • Features: CountVectorizer bag-of-words (sparse word counts).
  • Model: XGBoost classifier trained on Alexa review feedback labels (binary).
  • Visuals: distribution chart + word clouds + top tokens for interpretability.
